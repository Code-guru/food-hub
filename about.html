ABOUT US

Welcome to FoodHub Inc, your number one source for for food delivery. We're dedicated to providing you the very best of delivery, with an emphasis on healthy food, fast delivery time, and our customers satisfaction.
Founded in 2022 by Emmanuel Obinna, FoodHub Inc has come a long way from its beginnings in Abia State. When Emmanuel Obinna first started out, his passion for providing people with healthy food help drove him to start his own business.
We hope you enjoy our products as much as we enjoy offering them to you.

OUR MISSION

Our mission is to provide healthy meal service for those who are challenged to find either the time or energy to shop & cook or those who are always busy with work.
